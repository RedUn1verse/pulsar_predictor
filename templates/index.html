Pulsar Star Predictor











A pulsar is a collapsed star that was not quite big enough to form a black hole.
Instead it becomes a fast rotating neutron star that is of considerable scientific interest
as probes of space-time, the inter-stellar medium, and states of matter.
The predictions of this model are based off of DM-SNR curves built from the readings of different detections of pulsar candidates.

The dataset for this model was obtained from https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/HTRU2
